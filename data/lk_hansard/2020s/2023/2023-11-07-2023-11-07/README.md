Although details about the officers of the Sri Lanka Principals Service, Sri Lanka Teacher Advisers Service and the Sri Lanka Teachers Service who had retired from 1997 to date and the details of those who obtained the Widows/Widowers and Orphans' Pension have been asked for by this Question not for Oral Answers, our Ministry does not have the said details and were requested for in writing from the Department of Pensions. The Department of Pensions has also informed us that details so graded relating to each Service category cannot be given although they have details about the pensioners. Also, they have proposed to get the relevant details from the Provincial Departments of Education and the Zonal Education Offices.

Accordingly, details about the officers of the Sri Lanka Principals Service, the Sri Lanka Teacher Advisers Service and the Sri Lanka Teachers Service who had retired from 1997 to date and the details of those who had obtained the Widows/Widowers and Orphans' Pension have been asked for from the Zonal Education Offices. However, it has been informed that they do not have the details about the officers who were sent on retirement from the year 1997 and that they do not receive information about those who obtain the Widows/Widowers and Orphans' Pension.

Consequently, it is not possible to give a complete Answer for this Question at this juncture. Even if we requested for additional time to get down the details, we were not able to receive an adequate answer for that.
