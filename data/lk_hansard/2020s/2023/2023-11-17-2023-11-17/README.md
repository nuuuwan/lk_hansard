Sir, I hope we would have a debate on this Judgment later on.

Under the Land Development Ordinance, permits were issued to farmers for paddy lands in Thottachchurungiwaddai situated in Malwatta 02 Grama Niladhari Division in the Divisional Secretary's Division of Samanthurai in the Ampara District way back in 1964. Due to the conflict situation that prevailed in the country, the farmers who originally owned those lands were forced to vacate them, abandoning their paddy cultivation. They were displaced and left the Thottachchurungiwaddai area to other areas. Since then, people in the Uhana area have encroached the lands and continue to cultivate them to date.
