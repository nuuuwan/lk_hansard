( ) (The Hon. Seyed Ali Zahir Moulana) Hon. Speaker, I take this opportunity to second the Adjournment Motion moved by the Hon. S.M.M. Muszhaaraff regarding the voluntary teachers who have been serving in the North and the East.

During the very difficult time when the entire civil administration and various other services were at a stand-still and normalcy was not prevalent, these people came forward and were teaching voluntarily. The Hon. Muszhaaraff mentioned about the Ampara District. But, not only in the Ampara District, but it happened in the Batticaloa and Trincomalee Districts as well. I must make special mention here that out of those recruits, those who served as temporary teachers in the Northern Province were absorbed into the Northern Province Teachers Ser-vices upon a Cabinet Decision initiated in the year 2019 by the then State Minister of Education, the Hon. (Mrs.) Vijayakala Maheswaran. But, unfortunately, those who were working as volunteer teachers in the Eastern Prov-ince were excluded, they were not absorbed. So, it is very correct to be brought up by our Colleague, the Hon. S.M.M. Muszhaaraff and I take pleasure in seconding the Motion.
