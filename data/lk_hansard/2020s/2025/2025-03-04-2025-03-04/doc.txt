.. ( .. ) (The Hon. G.G. Ponnambalam) Hon. Presiding Member, I wish to raise three issues very quickly. The first one is this. The suboffices of the local government bodies in the Northern Province have been permited to issue building permits, name change of titles, certificates of compliance, subdivision of lands, amalgamation of lands and street line certificates. So, if that is the case, then you must intervene in this regard as well.

The other matter, Hon. Minister and Hon. Presiding Member, is that the Chavakachcheri Divisional Secretariat is a quite cumbersome Secretariat. The land area that Divisional Secretariat is supposed to cover is quite enormous. Therefore, there has been a long-time request - I remember, as a Member of Parliament in 2001, I myself making that written request to the Ministry of Public Administration - to somehow divide that Divisional Secretariat into two geographical regions basing Chavakachcheri, as it stands now, and also by creating a new Divisional Secretariat region centering Kodikamam. That must be done.

Finally, Hon. Presiding Member, I would like to refer to a matter which my Colleague who spoke just before me raised regarding the Kalmunai-North Divisional Secretariat. The Kalmunai-North Divisional Secretariat has been recognized since 1989. It was created as an AGA Division and then, by Cabinet Memoranda dated 17/3/1993 and 31/3/1993, the Ministry of Public Administration upgraded it to two Divisional Secretariats and approval was given by Cabinet Memorandum dated 09/07/1993. Since then, Hon. Minister, there have been interferences, unfortunately, due to political reasons, where the Kalmunai-North Divisional Secretariat has not been able to function fully. Literally and systematically, political interference caused it to not have an Accountant and also several parts of the Secretariat unable to function. Several of its current functions are gradually trying to be shifted to the Kalmunai-South Divisional Secretariat. During the previous Government, there was a talk that it was going to be downgraded to a suboffice. That must be stopped, Hon. Minister. That is what your Government came into power for. The decision regarding that Divisional Secretariat was taken in 1989 and it has been in practice. But, due to political interference, the functioning of this Secretariat has been scuttled.
