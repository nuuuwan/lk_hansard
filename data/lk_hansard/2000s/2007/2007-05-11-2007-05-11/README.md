Chairmans Office Peoples Bank November 22, 2006 Revision of payment of Peoples Bank pension Scheme - B.P. No. 411/96 The Board approved the proposal to revise the existing Pension Scheme of the Peoples Bank and adopt the following scheme with effectfrom the date of approval of the Board paper. (1) Bank employee who has completed 10 years of pensionable service but less than 30 years will be eligible to receive monthly pension on the basis of 90% of his salary at retirement as follows : (a) Commuted pension and (b) The reduced monthly pension (i. e. 80% of the last drawn salary) from the date of retirement at the age of 55 years. He will be entitled to the restoration of the unreduced monthly pension (90% of salary) on completion of 120 months from the date of commencement of the reduced monthly pension. (2) A Bank employee who has completed 30 years of sevice and over will be eligible to receive a Pension calculated on the basis of 90% of his salary at retirement as follows : (a) Commuted pension (b) The un-reduced monthly pension (90% of salary at retirement) notwithstanding the fact that he has drawn a commuted pension on retirement. I hereby certify that the foregoing is a true extract of the Minutes of the Meeting of the Board of Directors of the Peoples Bank held on 07th June 1996 at its Head Office in Colombo.
