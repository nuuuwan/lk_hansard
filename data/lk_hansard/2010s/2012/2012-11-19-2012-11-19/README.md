It is about the power of taxation of Parliament. We generally follow the rule that all taxation should, as far as possible, be general. You can increase taxation; you can reduce taxation. Having said that, I must say that I am concerned about some of the proposals that have been coming here, where we are trying to create space for niche markets - for a very small number of individuals - and the consequence is bad. I will give you one example. You have now changed the criteria for non-residency to 183 days. That is welcome. I do not think any country has 365 days to be a non-resident.
