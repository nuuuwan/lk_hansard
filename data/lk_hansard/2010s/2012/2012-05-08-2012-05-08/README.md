A simple instance of what I mean can be seen in the university sector. Many years ago, when there were grave problems at the Sabaragamuwa University, with massive amounts being transferred from the Recurrent Expenditure Account to the Capital Expenditure Account and then squandered, I suggested that we should make the accounts available to the students. We need to make sure that all institutions that have received public funding provide information to the public as to what they have done. In
