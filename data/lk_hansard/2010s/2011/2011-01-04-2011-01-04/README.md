There are areas in which the Minister in charge of Local Government and Provincial Councils comes into play. That is, in dissolution, in appointing special Commissioners and so on and so forth. Now, when that is the case, as I said, the President was well advised to circulate it to the Provincial Councils. We raised an issue in court with regard to the non-consultation of the provisions of this Bill with the

Northern Provincial Council, because there is no Northern Provincial Council. The Government took a dual approach to the problem.
